Our Story

Zimnyaya Skazka, meaning “Winter’s Tale,” was born in 2025 with a vision to bring the soul of Russian hospitality to life. Inspired by snow-dusted evenings and hearthside meals, we blend old-world charm with modern sophistication.

What We Cherish

  • Authentic Flavors: Every dish echoes timeless Russian traditions.
  • Heartfelt Ambience: A cozy, inviting space where every guest feels at home.
  • Locally Sourced: We celebrate quality with ingredients from trusted regional farms.

Meet the Heart of Our Kitchen

Chef Elena Ivanovna reimagines her grandmother’s treasured recipes with artistry and love, delivering plates filled with history and soul.

Manager Alexei brings grace and warmth to every guest interaction, making sure each visit feels like a return to family.
